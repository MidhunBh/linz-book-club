Linz English Book Club

A community for readers in Linz who enjoy discussing books and ideas in English.

About the Club

The Linz English Book Club brings together people from different backgrounds who enjoy reading and discussing books in English. Our goal goes beyond books — it's also about meeting new people, exchanging perspectives, and exploring ideas.

In the past two years, our club has been a welcoming community where members from around the world come together and find their crowd, where every voice can be heard.

Who Can Join

Anyone who can communicate in English and enjoys reading or meeting new people is welcome. You do not need to finish the book before joining a discussion.

  • Maximum of 20 participants per discussion
  • Open to international members and locals
  • Supportive and open-minded atmosphere

Where We Meet

The monthly meetings take place at Thalia Linz – Landstraße. We'll let you know the date and time beforehand.

Discussion Groups

Two groups currently exist:

  • Main Reading Group

    We explore different books across genres and eras.

  • Fantasy Reading Group

    Focused on fantasy literature and immersive world-building.
